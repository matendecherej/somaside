Our Story

Born from a belief that every voice deserves a stage.

Soma Side began as a simple question: why are African narratives so hard to find online? We built the answer, a platform where folktales sit beside modern poetry, and traditional songs echo alongside contemporary prose, all while being a little closer to our roots than before.

"Storytelling is the thread that stitches communities together."
Who we are

Where African stories find their home.

Soma Side is a living archive and creative stage, a place where poets, storytellers, and songwriters across Africa publish, connect, and resonate.

Why Soma Side

A platform built for the storytellers, the curious and the dreamers.

No noise. No clutter. Just stories that matter, surfaced for readers who care. We put the work first, always.
